Our Apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All app functionality runs locally on your device and no user data is sent to our servers.

Our Apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Since our Apps do not collect user data, the risk is minimal. Third-party services such as Google AdMob apply their own security standards to data they collect.

You can limit ad personalization by going to your device settings and disabling "Ad Personalization" or resetting your Advertising ID.

Bazı uygulamalarımız reklam gösterimi için Google AdMob hizmetini kullanmaktadır. Google AdMob, reklam kişiselleştirmesi amacıyla bazı verileri toplayabilir. Bu veriler Google'ın kendi gizlilik politikası kapsamında işlenir.

  • Google Gizlilik Politikası: policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob tarafından toplanabilecek veriler:

  • Cihaz tanımlayıcıları (Reklam ID)
  • IP adresi
  • Uygulama kullanım verileri
